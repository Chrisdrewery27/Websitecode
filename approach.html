Our Approach

We designed each program on the foundation of five strategic pillars:

Attraction: We employ innovative initiatives to spark participants' curiosity about the tech industry.

Exposure: We expose participant to the amazing world of technology and coding.

Engagement: We keep participants engaged by fostering more in-depth learning of principles and concepts.

Ownership: We help participants develop a sense of ownership of their new skill set by providing opportunities for them to develop real technology solutions.

Empowerment: We empower participants to inspire others to explore tech careers.
